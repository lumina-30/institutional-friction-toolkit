# Gradual Autonomy Normalization Check (Reference) ｜ 段階的自律性正常化チェック（参照）

This document does not propose prohibition, regulation, or intervention.

It provides descriptive questions intended to clarify whether
incremental automation or autonomy expansion
is becoming institutionally normalized
without explicit review of retained human authority.

---

When reviewing gradual system expansion, consider:

- Has decision scope expanded without explicit re-authorization?
- Has human override authority remained operational in practice?
- Has operational reliance increased faster than oversight adaptation?
- Are new dependencies forming without explicit accountability mapping?
- Is convenience replacing deliberate review?



Boundary responsibility and anti-bypass questions:

- Has responsibility for friction design, operation, evidence preservation, verification, and correction remained explicitly assigned as autonomy expanded?
- Has increased reliance made human refusal, correction, recovery, or re-entry practically unavailable before irreversibility?
- Is friction symmetric across the relevant institution, market, sector, procurement, audit, or governance layer?
- Could the autonomy expansion bypass review through incremental updates, outsourcing, opaque configuration changes, entity replacement, or responsibility diffusion?
- Were records preserved before affected humans or institutions lost voice, position, evidence access, or practical re-entry capacity?

境界責任と迂回防止に関する質問：

- 自律性の拡大に伴い、摩擦の設計・運用・証拠保存・検証・是正の責任主体は明示されたままか。
- 依存の増大によって、不可逆化前の人間の拒否・修正・回復・再参入は実効不能になっていないか。
- 摩擦は関連する制度・市場・分野・調達・監査・統治の層で対称的か。
- 自律性拡大は、段階的更新・外注化・不透明な設定変更・法人変更・責任拡散によってレビューを迂回できるか。
- 影響を受ける人間または制度が声・地位・証拠アクセス・実効的な再参入能力を失う前に、記録は保存されていたか。

---

This checklist is descriptive and non-prescriptive.
It is intended only to clarify whether gradual normalization
is occurring without explicit institutional acknowledgment.
