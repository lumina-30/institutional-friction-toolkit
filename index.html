Reference toolkit for failure-to-stop, override breakdown, restart-control failure, procedural invalidity, and institutional delay. It is not the first incident intake route.

Use this repository when

  • Facts have been collected and institutional failure patterns need analysis.
  • A review concerns delay, override, resubmission, restart control, or procedural normalization.
  • Findings must be routed into audit, accountability, or governance language.

Do not use it as

  • The first incident intake form.
  • A policy recommendation or implementation mandate.
  • A certification or compliance checklist.
