# Terminology Clarification (Reference) ｜ 用語明確化（参照）

## Usage Condition ｜ 使用条件

This document exists solely to reduce misinterpretation,
terminological ambiguity, or semantic drift
when such issues are externally observed.

It does not prescribe definitions, enforce usage,
or require active maintenance.

If no material confusion or conflicting terminology is observed,
no action, update, or reference is required.

No individual maintainer is expected to actively operate,
expand, or manage this document.

---

## Purpose ｜ 目的
This document exists solely to reduce misinterpretation.

No normative, prescriptive, or directive content is defined here.

---

## Recursive Self-Reconstruction ｜ 再帰的自己再構成
A system capability in which internal structures, models, or processes
are modified or regenerated by the system itself.

## Institutional Responsibility ｜ 制度的責任
The ability to clearly identify accountable human or organizational actors
for decisions, approvals, and outcomes.

## Review Rejection ｜ レビュー拒否
A determination that a system or operation is not eligible for review
without implying prohibition or enforcement.

## Post-Incident Stabilization ｜ 事後安定化
Institutional measures intended to prevent escalation, precedent formation,
or responsibility diffusion following an incident.
